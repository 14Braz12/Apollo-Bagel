The Process

Made the
right way.

Apollo is a new-school bagel shop rooted in old-school technique. Naturally fermented, boiled bagels made fresh every morning, served with a carefully curated menu featuring best-in-class producers.

Designed for the pace of New York life — simple flavors, high standards, and exceptional service. Reimagining what the local bagel shop can be.

01

Natural Fermentation

Live cultures, long proof, real tang. No shortcuts — just time doing its work.

02

Boiled, Then Baked

The original technique. Kettle-boiled for chew, oven-baked for the perfect crust.

03

Fresh Every Morning

Watch them come out of the oven. Never day-old. Always worth the wait.
